About Us

We are a government high school located in the serene and picturesque village of "Bhotanta Mahitara" in the northeastern state of Assam, India. Established in 1989, our school has been a beacon of knowledge and education for over three decades.

Nestled amidst the beauty of nature in a remote rural area, Phinguagarh High School takes pride in its commitment to environmental conservation. Our school has garnered a well-deserved reputation for promoting and instilling a sense of environmental awareness among our students. We believe in nurturing responsible citizens who understand the importance of protecting and preserving our natural resources.

At Phinguagarh High School, we provide a nurturing and inclusive learning environment that empowers students to grow academically, socially, and emotionally. Our dedicated team of educators is passionate about imparting knowledge and skills that go beyond textbooks, encouraging holistic development and critical thinking.
